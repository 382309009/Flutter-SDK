WarningCode enum

Warning codes occur when the SDK encounters an error that may be recovered automatically. These are only notifications, and can generally be ignored. For example, when the SDK loses connection to the server, the SDK reports the OpenChannelTimeout(106) warning and tries to reconnect automatically. See WarningCode.OpenChannelTimeout @enum {number}
